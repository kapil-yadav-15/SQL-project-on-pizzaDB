# SQL-project-on-pizzaDB

Performed comprehensive SQL-based analysis on a pizza sales database to calculate key business KPIs, identify revenue trends, and rank product performance.
The project covers end-to-end data querying from basic aggregations to advanced ranking queries, providing actionable insights for menu optimization, 
inventory planning, and sales strategy using structured SQL.

Revenue and quantity rankings reveal clear best and worst performing products for menu optimization
Category and size-based percentage breakdowns support targeted pricing and promotional strategies
Monthly category filtering enables seasonal demand tracking and inventory management.
